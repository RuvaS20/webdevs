Our Mission

Msasa Academy is a dedicated climate change education platform, providing a hub for students and teachers to engage with quizzes, discussion forums, and visualizing climate data and news. Our goal is to empower the next generation to take meaningful action towards a sustainable future.

Our Features

01

Climate Data

We allow you to visualise data on climate change and realtime news from the Guardian.

02

Interactive Learning

Engage with our interactive quizzes that make learning about climate change both fun and effective.

03

Community Forums

Join discussions with other learners and experts to share ideas and solutions for climate action.
